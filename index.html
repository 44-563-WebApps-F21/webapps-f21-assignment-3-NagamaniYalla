Andaman
Andaman is a beutiful island with beautiful beaches and a lot of water sport activities and is located in India. Mainly Rk beach ,Ross Island and havelock island are my favorite places in Andaman.My favorite things to do there are Paragliding and Scuba diving which are both adventurous and fun.

Directions form Maryville to Andaman

  1. Take a Taxi from Maryville to Kansas
  2. Board the Flight from Kansas to Chennai,India
  3. From Chennai take the below route to our final destination
    1. Take Grand Southern Trunk Rd, N Fort Rd and Beach Rd to W Madha Church St in Chetty Thottam
    2. Head south on Raja Muthiah Rd toward Chennai - Villupuram - Trichy - Kanyakumari Rd/Grand Southern Trunk Rd/Poonamallee High Rd
    3. Turn left after National Durbar Restaurant (on the right)
    4. Continue onto General Hospital Rd
    5. General Hospital Rd turns left and becomes Muthuswamy Rd
    6. Continue onto N Fort Rd
    7. Continue onto Beach Rd/Rajaji Rd
    8. Turn right onto Surya Narayana St
    9. Take the ferry to Port Blair
  4. We have arrived at the beuatiful island
